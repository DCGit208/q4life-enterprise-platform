Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about using and maintaining your Anker SOLIX power station.

How do I use a solar charger to charge my system?

Connect solar panels via XT60 or MC4 connectors. System automatically optimizes solar input. F3800 accepts up to 2400W solar - use multiple panels for fastest charging.

How long can the C1000 power a device?

Runtime depends on device wattage. Examples: Laptop (50W) = 18hrs, Fridge (150W) = 6hrs, TV (100W) = 9hrs. Use app to monitor real-time consumption.

Why does charging take longer sometimes?

Charging speed varies based on input source (AC vs solar), temperature, battery level. System slows charging above 80% to protect battery longevity.

Can I charge with AC and solar simultaneously?

Yes! F3800 supports dual charging. Combine grid power + solar for ultra-fast recharge. C-series models have single input (AC or solar, not both).
