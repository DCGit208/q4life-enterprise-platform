Why Solar Roof vs Solar Panels?

Choose Solar Roof if:
  • Building new home or replacing roof
  • Aesthetics are priority (no visible panels)
  • Want strongest roof protection available
  • Premium architectural design
Choose Solar Panels if:
  • Existing roof in good condition
  • Lower upfront investment
  • Faster installation (days vs weeks)
  • Easier to expand later
