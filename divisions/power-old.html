Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about backup power systems, batteries, and installation.

How long will the battery last? (Lifespan)

EcoFlow batteries use LFP chemistry rated for 3,000-6,000+ cycles to 80% capacity. That's 10-15 years of daily use. Tesla Powerwall lasts 10+ years. Much longer than traditional generators.

Can I expand the system later if my needs grow?

Yes! DELTA Pro is modular — add extra batteries (up to 25kWh), solar panels, or even additional DELTA Pro units. We design systems with future expansion in mind.

How long does installation take?

Portable systems (DELTA 2/Pro): 2-4 hours for basic setup.
Whole-home systems (Ocean Pro/Powerwall): 1-3 days depending on complexity.
Solar integration: Add 2-5 days for panel installation.

What maintenance is required?

Nearly zero! Modern lithium batteries don't need water, fuel, or regular servicing like generators. Just keep firmware updated (we help with this) and dust off solar panels occasionally.

Why EcoFlow instead of cheap Chinese batteries from AliExpress?

Safety: EcoFlow has UL, CE, FCC certifications. Cheap batteries can overheat or fail dangerously.
Warranty: 5-10 year coverage vs none.
Support: We provide local installation, troubleshooting, and service. AliExpress? Good luck.
Performance: Certified specs vs fictional ratings.
