Vehicle Trading

Transaction infrastructure connecting buyers, sellers, and financing partners. Commission-based marketplace with zero inventory risk.

Core Model: Transaction Facilitation, Not Inventory

We don't buy or hold vehicles. We facilitate transactions between parties and earn commissions on:

  • Sales Commissions: 3-7% on every transaction
  • Financing Referrals: 5-12% on loan origination
  • Insurance Brokerage: 15-25% annually on premiums
  • Registration Services: $50-$150 service fee per vehicle

Zero Capital Model: No inventory purchases, no storage costs, no depreciation risk. Pure transaction infrastructure.
