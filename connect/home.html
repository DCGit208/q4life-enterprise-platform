Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do I need to buy Starlink hardware?
    Yes. You order the Starlink kit ($599) directly from Starlink.com. We install it for you.
  • How long does installation take?
    Basic setup: 2-4 hours. Premium with mesh WiFi: 4-6 hours. Power+ systems: Full day.
  • What speeds can I expect?
    Starlink delivers 50-200 Mbps download, 10-20 Mbps upload. Sufficient for streaming, work, and gaming.
  • What's included in support?
    Remote troubleshooting, network optimization tips, and guidance on Starlink subscription issues.
  • Does weather affect Starlink?
    Heavy rain/storms may cause brief slowdowns, but Starlink works in most weather conditions.
