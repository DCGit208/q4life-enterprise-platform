The Revenue Stacking Model

How Q4-LIFE extracts maximum value from distributed energy systems

The Traditional Energy Model: A customer buys electricity. The utility earns fixed per-kWh revenue. Done.

The Q4-LIFE Revenue Stacking Model: One battery installation generates revenue from six concurrent streams. Same facility, same customer, multiple revenue channels. This is why distributed energy is the future.

The Result? A 100 kWh hotel battery system in Douala generates $1.2M+ over 25 years — for the utility, for Q4-LIFE, and for the customer.

The Six Revenue Streams

SLA Guarantees

Customer pays monthly fee for guaranteed uptime (99.5%–99.99%). No energy consumed, just availability.

$5k–$15k/month

Demand Charge Reduction

AI shifts peak loads to off-peak hours. Customer splits savings 60/40 with Q4-LIFE. Largest monthly utility line item.

$3k–$8k/month

Energy Arbitrage

Buy electricity when cheap (midnight, low demand). Sell back when expensive (evening peak). Pure margin.

$2k–$5k/month

Solar PPAs

Q4-LIFE installs solar. Customer pays fixed $/kWh (inflation protection). 20-year revenue certainty.

$4k–$10k/month

Grid Services

ENEO compensates for frequency regulation, demand response, capacity reserve. Virtual power plant value.

$1k–$4k/month

Microgrid Control

If system scales to campus/industrial park: turnkey ownership or $5k–$20k/month O&M fees.

$5k–$20k/month

Sensitivity Analysis: What If?

Real-world outcomes depend on diesel price, grid services compensation, and system utilization. Here's how the model scales under different scenarios:

Scenario: Diesel Prices Rise 50% (High Probability)

Customer SLA Willingness: +$4k/month (+40% pricing power)

When diesel becomes $1.50+/liter, customers view backup power as essential, not optional. Price elasticity shifts.

25-Year Profit Impact: +$1.2M (25 years × $4k/month × 0.5 probability)

Scenario: Grid Stability Improves (Lower Probability)

SLA Fee Reduction: -$2k/month (-20% demand)

If ENEO stabilizes grid with new generation, customers view uptime as less critical. But arbitrage demand INCREASES.

Arbitrage Revenue Increase: +$3k/month (30% larger spread)
Net 25-Year Impact: -$100k (breakeven trade-off)

Scenario: Grid Services Expand (HIGHEST PROBABILITY)

Grid Services Revenue: +$2k/month (regulatory ramp)

ENEO incentivizes frequency regulation and demand response. Aggregators like Q4-LIFE are critical to grid modernization.

25-Year Profit Upside: +$600k
