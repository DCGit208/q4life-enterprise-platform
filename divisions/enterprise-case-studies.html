Regional Bank Headquarters

Yaoundé, Cameroon | Financial Services | Deployed 2023

Facility Type 12-story office + data center
Peak Load 500 kW
Daily Consumption 3 MWh
System Size 250 kWh battery + 150 kW solar

Challenge

The bank experienced 2–3 grid outages per week lasting 30 minutes to 3 hours. Each outage disrupted customer service, ATM operations, and data processing. Emergency diesel generators were costly, noisy, and unreliable. The bank needed guaranteed uptime (99.5%+ SLA) and wanted to reduce operating costs.

Q4-LIFE Solution

Hybrid solar + battery + grid system with UPS redundancy. Rooftop solar charges batteries during the day; battery backup activates within 100 milliseconds of grid loss, ensuring uninterrupted power to critical systems (data center, ATM network, HVAC). On-site diesel generator serves as emergency reserve (rarely used). Full system automation via Q4-LIFE's control platform. 99.5% SLA guaranteed with monthly service credits for breaches.

Timeline: 6 months (design → commissioning) Total Investment: $400,000
OPEX Reduction 30% ($180k/year)
Diesel Fuel Eliminated $120k/year (fuel costs)
Generator Maintenance $30k/year (reduced staff, parts)
Avoided Outage Costs $300k+/year (lost productivity, customer churn)
Estimated Annual Benefit $620k+

Year 1 Results

✓ Grid Uptime: 99.97% achieved (zero unplanned outages)
✓ Energy Savings: $180k (OPEX reduction)
✓ Operational Efficiency: +15% (no cooling loss during outages)
✓ Staff Reduction: 2 FTE diesel technicians reassigned
✓ Brand Impact: "Most Reliable Financial Institution" messaging
✓ Payback Timeline: 3.2 years (faster than projected)
✓ 10-Year NPV: +$2.8M
"We went from managing power anxiety to predictable reliability. Every hour of power availability now translates directly to customer confidence and transaction completion. The investment pays for itself through avoided outage costs alone. That doesn't even account for the peace of mind."
— Chief Financial Officer, Bank HQ

Hospitality Portfolio

Douala, Cameroon | 3-Hotel Group | Deployed 2023

Portfolio 3 hotels (200 rooms total)
Peak Load (Combined) 200 kW
Daily Consumption 2 MWh
System Size 2 × 100 kWh batteries + rooftop solar

Challenge

Diesel generator noise complaints from guests. Unpredictable grid power degraded guest experience and hurt occupancy rates. Energy costs were 18–22% of operating budget. Competitors with reliable backup power were capturing market share.

Q4-LIFE Solution

Two modular battery systems (one per hotel cluster) plus rooftop solar panels. Smart load shedding ensures guest-facing systems (lighting, AC, hot water) never lose power, while non-critical systems (laundry, landscaping) shed load during grid outages. Result: silent, seamless backup. Demand charge management shifts peak loads to off-peak hours, reducing energy bills further.

Timeline: 4 months (phased rollout per hotel) Total Investment: $250,000
Diesel Usage Reduction 70% ($90k/year)
Peak Demand Shaving $45k/year
Generator Maintenance -$20k/year
Occupancy Improvement +3% = $180k/year (premium rate booking)
Annual Benefit $315k+

Year 1 Results

✓ Guest Satisfaction: +25% (silent backup, no power loss)
✓ Energy Cost Reduction: -$135k (65% diesel + demand charges)
✓ Maintenance Effort: -4 hours/week (reduced generator checks)
✓ Occupancy Rate: +3% year-over-year
✓ Premium Booking Rate: +8% (reliability as selling point)
✓ Payback Timeline: 3.1 years (fastest in portfolio)
✓ 15-Year NPV: +$2.2M
"Our guests notice the silence immediately. No generator noise. Power is always on. They stay longer and book premium suites. We went from justifying power as cost center to marketing it as a premium amenity. That was transformational for our business."
— General Manager, Hotel Group

Mining Operation

Eastern Cameroon | Remote Processing Plant | Deployed 2022

Facility Remote processing plant + camp
Peak Load 2 MW (24/7)
Daily Consumption 35 MWh
System Size 2 MWh battery + 3 MW solar farm

Challenge

Remote mining site located 60+ km from nearest grid. Fuel transport costs exceeded $2.1M/year. Diesel generators were unreliable in remote setting; frequent breakdowns halted processing. Operation needed 98%+ uptime to meet production targets and commodity market timing. Environmental concerns about diesel emissions in protected area.

Q4-LIFE Solution

Modular 2 MWh battery storage system + 3 MWp solar farm designed for 24/7 load averaging 80% capacity factor. System sized for daily recharge cycle using strong regional solar resource (5.5+ kWh/m²/day). Diesel backup generator kept for emergency only (< 5% utilization). Q4-LIFE owns and operates system under long-term energy services contract; mining company buys power at fixed $/kWh.

Timeline: 9 months (site prep + system build) Q4-LIFE Investment: $3.2M
Diesel Fuel Cost Eliminated $2.1M/year
Overall Energy Cost -55% vs. current
Production Efficiency +12% (stable power = better yields)
CO₂ Reduction 8,000 tons/year (marketable)
Mining Co. Annual Benefit $1.8M+

Year 1 Results (Q4-LIFE / Mining Co.)

✓ Uptime Achievement: 98.5% (target met; zero fuel-related outages)
✓ Fuel Elimination: $2.1M annual savings (mining co.)
✓ Production Yield: +12% due to power stability
✓ CO₂ Reduction: 8,000 tons/year (ESG marketing value)
✓ Q4-LIFE Revenue: $900k from energy sales + operations
✓ Q4-LIFE Payback: 4.5 years
