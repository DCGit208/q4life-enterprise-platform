DER Aggregation Strategy

Q4-LIFE builds a virtual power plant by aggregating distributed energy resources (batteries, solar, load control) across multiple customer sites. Individually, each site is small; collectively, they're a grid asset.

Technical Architecture

Each customer site runs Q4-LIFE's cloud-connected control platform. The central DER management platform sends real-time dispatch signals to coordinate:

  • Load Forecasting: Predict customer demand + solar production 24 hours ahead
  • Battery Dispatch: Charge during lowest-cost hours, discharge during peak or grid stress
  • Frequency Response: Detect grid anomalies and respond within 100ms (faster than ENEO infrastructure)
  • Demand Response: Reduce non-critical loads during grid emergencies (30-second notice)
