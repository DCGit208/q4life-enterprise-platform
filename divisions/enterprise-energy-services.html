Six Stacked Revenue Streams for Energy Independence & Optimization

Guaranteed Uptime Contract

Service Description

Q4-LIFE guarantees 99.5% power availability via documented Service-Level Agreements (SLAs). Our hybrid battery + grid system ensures zero unplanned outages for critical loads. 24/7 network monitoring, rapid response protocols (within 30 minutes), and scheduled maintenance windows.

Revenue Model

Fixed Monthly Service Fee
Client pays recurring fee (~$5,000–$15,000/month depending on facility size & load). Q4-LIFE guarantees uptime; service credits apply for any breaches.

Typical ROI

30% OPEX Reduction by eliminating unplanned downtime costs (lost productivity, emergency repairs, temporary power rentals).
Payback: 2–3 years through operational efficiency gains.

Implementation Timeline

Month 1: Energy audit, system design, technical requirements
Months 2–3: Hardware procurement, installation, testing
Month 4+: Live operation, SLA monitoring, optimization

Technical Requirements

System Size 100 kWh – 1 MWh depending on critical load
Response Time < 100 milliseconds (uninterruptible)
Monitoring 24/7 remote + on-site
SLA Credit 5–10% monthly fee per hour of missed SLA

Perfect For

Data centers, hospitals, banking headquarters, telecommunications hubs, e-commerce fulfillment centers. Any facility where even 1 hour of downtime costs > $50,000.

Demand Charge Management

Service Description

Q4-LIFE's AI-powered battery storage system automatically shifts loads, avoiding peak-hour energy pricing. By charging during off-peak (cheap) hours and discharging during peak demand, we reduce your highest-cost energy charges by 20–40%.

Revenue Model

Shared Savings Model (60% Client / 40% Q4-LIFE)
We monitor demand charges, Q4-LIFE optimizes storage dispatch, and both parties share the reduction. Total peak charge reduction typically ranges $50k–$200k/year depending on facility.

Typical ROI

$100k–$500k/year in savings depending on load profile and peak tariff rates.
Payback: 2–4 years on battery system cost.

Implementation Timeline

Week 1–2: Load profile analysis, peak period identification
Weeks 3–4: AI algorithm training, baseline optimization
Month 2+: Live optimization, daily reporting, savings tracking

Technical Requirements

Battery Capacity Sized for 1–3 hours of peak load
AI Dispatch Real-time forecasting + utility pricing data
Integration BMS + utility meter integration
Monitoring Automated daily savings reports

Perfect For

Manufacturing plants, cold storage warehouses, e-commerce fulfillment, industrial processing. Facilities with peak demand charges > 30% of total energy bill.

Energy Arbitrage & Trading

Service Description

Q4-LIFE operates battery storage systems as profit centers, buying low-cost grid power during off-peak hours and selling during peak demand windows. We can also participate in utility grid balancing services, generating additional revenue from frequency regulation and capacity reserves.

Revenue Model

Q4-LIFE Earns | Facility Benefits
Q4-LIFE monetizes energy arbitrage + grid services. Host facility receives reduced demand charges as secondary benefit. Win-win arrangement.

Typical ROI

$30k–$100k/year arbitrage revenue per 500 kWh system.
Additional $20k–$50k/year from grid services (via Eneo contracts in Cameroon).
Payback: 3–5 years on Q4-LIFE side.

Implementation Timeline

Month 1: Regulatory & grid operator approvals (Eneo integration)
Month 2: Trading algorithm development & backtesting
Month 3+: Live trading, daily optimization, quarterly settlements

Technical Requirements

System Size 500+ kWh for viable arbitrage
Grid Connection Direct metering, bidirectional power flow
Software Real-time market data, predictive analytics
Regulation Cameroon MINEE DER aggregation license

Perfect For

Large industrial facilities, utility partners, renewable energy producers, municipal governments. Any site with 500+ kWh storage and grid connection.

Commercial Solar + Storage PPAs

Service Description

Q4-LIFE owns, installs, and operates solar panels + battery storage systems on client facilities for 15–20 year Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs). Clients buy renewable energy at fixed $/kWh rates, locked in for inflation protection. Q4-LIFE manages all operations, maintenance, and optimization.

Revenue Model

Energy Sales PPA ($0.08–$0.12 USD/kWh)
Client buys all renewable output at fixed rate (negotiated based on site solar resource). Q4-LIFE earns margin on generation cost + operations.

Typical ROI

Client saves 30–50% on electricity costs vs. grid-only or diesel hybrid.
Payback: 6–8 years on soft costs; long-term hedge value much higher.
Q4-LIFE earns: $50k–$200k/year per MWh system.

Implementation Timeline

Month 1: Solar resource assessment, load profiling
Months 2–3: Engineering design, PPA negotiation, financing
Months 4–6: Permitting, installation, grid interconnection
Month 7+: Operational, meter-based billing, continuous optimization

Technical Requirements

System Size 50–500 kWp solar + 50–200 kWh storage
Roof/Ground Space Depends on kWp (typically 2–5 m²/kWp)
Grid Connection Standard interconnection (15–25 days Eneo)
Contract Term 15–20 years (energy security)

Perfect For

Real estate developers, hospitality groups, government facilities, manufacturing plants. Any tenant/owner with steady daytime or 24/7 loads and roof/ground space for solar.

Grid Services & Frequency Regulation

Service Description

Q4-LIFE's distributed battery network provides critical grid stability services: frequency regulation, voltage support, emergency capacity reserves. We bid these services to utilities (Eneo in Cameroon) and regional grid operators, generating recurring revenue. Host facility benefits from grid support contracts and potential emergency backup capability.

Revenue Model

Grid Service Contracts + Performance Bonuses
Q4-LIFE contracts with utilities for frequency regulation services (~$50k–$150k/year per 500 kWh system). Revenue split or dedicated capacity reservation for host facility's emergency use.

Typical ROI

Host facility: $20k–$50k/year from shared grid service revenue or emergency reserve discount.
Q4-LIFE: $40k–$120k/year per system from grid operator contracts.
Cameroon utility (Eneo): Grid stability benefit worth $100k+/year in deferred infrastructure.

Implementation Timeline

Month 1: Utility discussions, regulatory framework approval
Month 2: Telemetry integration, SCADA connections
Month 3+: Service provision, monthly settlements with Eneo

Technical Requirements

Response Capability < 100 ms (automated frequency support)
Grid Connection Direct metering + SCADA integration
Regulatory DER aggregator license + service agreements
Availability 99.5% uptime (standby reserve capacity)

Perfect For

Strategic urban locations near grid substations, industrial parks with multiple facilities, utility partners piloting DER programs. Any site with 300+ kWh storage willing to provide emergency grid support.

Perfect for campuses, industrial parks, remote facilities, and real estate developments. Microgrids can island from main grid during emergencies, providing complete energy independence.

Revenue Model

Turnkey Ownership or Operations & Maintenance (O&M) Model
Q4-LIFE can own & operate full microgrid (client buys renewable energy via PPA), or client owns hardware and Q4-LIFE provides O&M services ($5k–$20k/month).

Typical ROI

50–70% reduction in total energy costs through solar + optimization + grid services.
Payback: 5–8 years (faster for O&M-only models).

Implementation Timeline

Months 1–2: Masterplanning, load assessment, renewable resource analysis
Months 3–4: Engineering design, permitting, financing
Months 5–8: Installation, controls integration, testing
Month 9+: Operational, optimization, performance reporting

Technical Requirements

System Scale 100 kWp – 5 MWp solar + proportional storage
Control Architecture Islanding-capable microgrid controller
Redundancy Multi-source generation + battery + grid backup
Integration Building automation, EV charging, load management

Perfect For

Real estate developers (new builds), university campuses, industrial parks, government facilities, remote mining/agricultural operations. Any multi-building complex wanting energy autonomy + cost control.
