Fleet Systems

B2B fleet management + driver-as-a-service programs. Stable recurring revenue from corporate contracts and driver leasing.

Core Business Model

Fleet Systems operates on two tracks:

1. Driver-as-a-Service (DaaS)

Vehicles leased to drivers who earn income from ride-hailing, delivery, or personal taxi services. Drivers pay weekly/monthly lease fees.

  • Revenue Model: $700-$900/vehicle/month
  • Driver Benefits: Access to income-generating asset without capital outlay
  • Our Services: Maintenance, insurance, GPS tracking, roadside assistance
  • Lease-to-Own Option: Drivers can purchase vehicle after 24-36 months

2. Corporate Fleet Contracts (B2B)

Long-term fleet management for NGOs, hotels, schools, enterprises. 12-24 month contracts with stable monthly revenue.

  • Revenue Model: $500-$800/vehicle/month + management fee
  • Client Benefits: No capital expense, predictable monthly cost, full-service management
  • Our Services: Vehicle procurement, maintenance, driver sourcing, GPS tracking, fuel management

Key Advantage: B2B contracts = predictable cash flow. 85%+ retention rate. Lower churn than consumer-facing businesses.

Driver-as-a-Service Program

Driver Qualification Criteria

  • Valid Driver's License: Minimum 2 years driving experience
  • Background Check: Criminal record + traffic violation screening
  • Income Verification: Minimum demonstrated monthly earnings capacity
  • Security Deposit: $300-$500 (refundable)
  • Training Program: Mandatory safety + customer service training (via Q4-Life Education)

Lease Agreement Structure

  • Weekly Lease: $180-$220/week ($720-$880/month)
  • Monthly Lease: $700-$850/month (discounted vs weekly)
  • Lease Term: 12-36 months
  • Included Services: Insurance, GPS tracking, roadside assistance, first maintenance
  • Excluded: Fuel, driver-caused damages, traffic fines

Driver Support Services

  • 24/7 Roadside Assistance: Emergency towing + battery jumpstart
  • Maintenance Network: Access to certified mechanics (discounted rates)
  • GPS Tracking: Fleet management + theft recovery
  • Income Analytics: Dashboard showing daily earnings, expenses, net profit
  • Lease-to-Own Path: Option to purchase vehicle after 24 months (residual value credit)

Driver Economics: Average ride-hailing driver earns $1,200-$1,800/month. After $800 lease + $400 fuel = $0-$600 net. Attractive for drivers without vehicle capital.
